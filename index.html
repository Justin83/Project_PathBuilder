Project PathBuilder

Biology Foundations

Guided homeschool-compatible biology study using OpenStax Concepts of Biology, weekly packets, labs, parent-supported assignments, and Tuesday concept check-ins.

Course Model

This course uses a weekly rhythm. Students complete selected readings and notebook work during the week, attend a Tuesday concept overview and activity session, and finish follow-up work at home. Parents remain the primary graders unless a separate arrangement is requested.

Parent Screening Note

Linked videos and web resources are provided to support weekly biology topics. Parents should preview videos and links according to their family’s standards before student use. Parents make the final suitability decision for their student.

Primary Text

The primary free textbook is OpenStax Concepts of Biology. Weekly packets will point students to selected sections instead of requiring full-chapter reading unless appropriate.
