Biology Foundations

A guided homeschool-compatible biology course using printed weekly packets, online resources, hands-on Tuesday activities, and selected readings from OpenStax Concepts of Biology.

Course Model

Biology Foundations is designed to support homeschool families with a steady weekly rhythm. Students complete reading, vocabulary, worksheets, notebook work, and review at home during the week. Tuesday meetings are used for concept review, questions, discussion, and hands-on activities or labs.

Primary Text

The primary free textbook is OpenStax Concepts of Biology. Families are not required to purchase a paper copy. Families who prefer a paper copy should use the OpenStax book page and its official print-copy option.
