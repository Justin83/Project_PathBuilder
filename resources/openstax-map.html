Primary Text

OpenStax Concepts of Biology will be used as the main free textbook resource. The course will use selected sections rather than assigning full chapters every week.

Planning Note

Evolutionary theory is not a major focus of this course. It may be referenced where relevant to classification, adaptation, biodiversity, or textbook context, but the course emphasis is biological literacy, observation, cells, heredity, organisms, ecology, and lab skills.
