# OpenStax Topic Map

Primary text: [OpenStax Concepts of Biology](https://openstax.org/details/books/concepts-biology)

This course will use selected sections rather than assigning full chapters every week.

## First Six-Week Planning Map

| Week | Course Topic | OpenStax Anchor | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Biology, observation, and course habits | Introduction to Biology | Use for science process, biology as study of life, and observation. |
| 2 | Characteristics of life | Introduction to Biology | Focus on properties shared by living things. |
| 3 | Chemistry of life | Chemistry of Life | Keep this practical and introductory. |
| 4 | Cells and microscopes | Cell Structure and Function | Focus on plant/animal cells and microscope use. |
| 5 | Membranes, diffusion, and osmosis | Cell Structure and Function | Focus on membranes and movement across boundaries. |
| 6 | Enzymes and life processes | How Cells Obtain Energy | Use metabolism/enzyme ideas without making the biochemistry too heavy. |

## Planning Note

Evolutionary theory is not a major focus of this course. It may be referenced when relevant to classification, adaptation, biodiversity, or textbook context, but the course emphasis is biological literacy, observation, cells, heredity, organisms, ecology, and lab skills.
