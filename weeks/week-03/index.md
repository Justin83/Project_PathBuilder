# Week 03 — Chemistry of Life

## Focus

Students learn why water, pH, carbon, and biological molecules matter in living things.

## Student Tasks

- Read the assigned OpenStax chemistry of life section when finalized.
- Define atom, molecule, pH, acid, base, carbohydrate, lipid, protein, and nucleic acid.
- Complete a food label or pH data activity.

## Tuesday Activity

Household-safe pH testing activity or food molecule comparison.
