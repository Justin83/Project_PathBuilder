# Week 02 — Characteristics of Life

## Focus

Students learn what living things have in common and practice supporting answers with evidence.

## Student Tasks

- Read the assigned OpenStax section when finalized.
- Define cell, metabolism, growth, reproduction, response, homeostasis, and adaptation.
- Complete the characteristics of life chart.

## Tuesday Activity

Living, nonliving, and once-living sorting activity.
