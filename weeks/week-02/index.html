Characteristics of Life

Focus

This week focuses on the shared characteristics of living things and how students can use evidence to explain whether something is living, nonliving, or once living.

Student Tasks

  • Define cell, metabolism, growth, reproduction, response, homeostasis, and adaptation.
  • Complete the characteristics of life chart.

Living, nonliving, and once-living sorting activity.
