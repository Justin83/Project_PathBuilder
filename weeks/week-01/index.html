Week 01

Biology, Observation, and Course Habits

Students learn what biology studies, how to set up a biology notebook, and how to distinguish observation from inference.

Back to course home

Focus

This week introduces the course rhythm and the basic habits students will use all semester: reading carefully, following directions, writing observations, and keeping a biology notebook.

Student Tasks

  • Set up a biology notebook or binder.
  • Read the assigned OpenStax introduction section when finalized.
  • Define biology, observation, inference, hypothesis, variable, and evidence.
  • Complete the observation activity sheet.

Tuesday Activity

Observation vs. inference activity using natural objects or classroom items.

At-Home Work

Reading, vocabulary, notebook setup, and worksheet completion happen during the week at home.
