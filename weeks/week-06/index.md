# Week 06 — Enzymes and Life Processes

## Focus

Students learn that living things use enzymes to help chemical reactions happen.

## Student Tasks

- Read the assigned OpenStax metabolism or energy section when finalized.
- Define enzyme, substrate, reaction, metabolism, and energy.
- Complete enzyme observation questions.

## Tuesday Activity

Catalase activity using potato or yeast and hydrogen peroxide.
