# Week 04 — Cells and Microscopes

## Focus

Students learn that cells are the basic unit of life and practice microscope safety and observation.

## Student Tasks

- Read the assigned OpenStax cell structure section when finalized.
- Define cell membrane, cytoplasm, nucleus, mitochondria, chloroplast, cell wall, and vacuole.
- Label plant and animal cell diagrams.

## Tuesday Activity

Microscope demonstration or lab using prepared slides or simple wet mounts.

## Parent Note

Please preview linked videos and websites before student use. Resources are recommended for course support, but parents make the final suitability decision.
