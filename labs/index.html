Working Lab Model

The default plan is for labs and hands-on activities to happen during the Tuesday co-op meeting. Reading, vocabulary, worksheets, notebook work, and review will normally happen during the week at home.

Some activities may require a short at-home follow-up if results need time to develop. These will be occasional and clearly explained in the weekly packet.

Suggested First Six Activities

  1. Observation vs. inference activity using natural objects or classroom items.
  2. Living, nonliving, and once-living sorting activity.
  3. pH testing or food label chemistry activity.
  4. Microscope observation of prepared slides or simple wet mounts.
  5. Gummy bear, egg, or dialysis osmosis activity.
  6. Catalase enzyme activity using potato or yeast and hydrogen peroxide.

Possible SCC Campus Lab Days

A campus microscope lab is recommended if offsite microscope access is limited. Other possible campus lab days may include biomolecules or enzymes, DNA extraction and genetics, and ecology or classification.

Supplies

The current lab supply list is a working plan, not a final purchase order. Supplies are to be determined and may change based on student count, meeting location, available equipment, cost, and parent feedback.
