# Biology Foundations: Sample Course Start Page

Welcome to Biology Foundations. This course is designed to help homeschool students learn biology through a weekly rhythm of reading, vocabulary, notebook work, discussion, and hands-on activities.

We will use OpenStax Concepts of Biology as the main free textbook. The instructor will provide weekly guidance so students are not left to figure out the textbook alone.

This course is not AP Biology and is not intended to replace the parent’s role in homeschooling. Parents remain the primary graders unless another arrangement is made. The instructor’s role is to provide structure, explain concepts, lead activities and labs, answer questions, and help students stay on track.

## Weekly Pattern

Before Tuesday, students complete assigned reading, vocabulary, and notebook work.

During Tuesday, the instructor reviews the major concept, answers questions, leads discussion, and introduces or completes an activity or lab.

After Tuesday, students complete follow-up work, lab reflections, or parent-graded review.

## What Students Bring

Students should bring a biology notebook or binder, pencil, assigned worksheet, and completed reading notes.

## Course Emphasis

The course emphasizes careful reading, following directions, vocabulary, observation, data recording, short explanations, lab safety, and consistent weekly habits.

## Parent Note

Parents are encouraged to preview linked videos and websites before student use. Resources are recommended for course support, but parents make the final suitability decision.
