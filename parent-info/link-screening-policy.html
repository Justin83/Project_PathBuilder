Parent Decision-Making

Some course materials may include links to videos, OpenStax readings, web articles, simulations, lab demonstrations, or other supplemental resources. These resources are offered in good faith to support the biology topic for the week.

Parents should decide whether each outside resource is appropriate for their own family.

Links and QR Codes

Course documents should work well from both paper and screen. Major online resources may include clickable links for digital use and QR codes for printed packets. Phones are not required; QR codes are included only as a convenience.

Resource Labels

Weekly resource boxes may identify resources as required, recommended, or optional. Optional resources may be skipped or replaced by parents.
