Welcome

Biology Foundations is designed to help homeschool students learn biology through a steady weekly pattern of reading, vocabulary, notebook work, discussion, and hands-on activities.

The course uses OpenStax Concepts of Biology as the main free textbook. Weekly packets will guide students to the sections and activities they need.

How Each Week Works

  1. Read the assigned section and complete the vocabulary or notebook work.
  2. Bring your packet, notes, and questions to the Tuesday meeting.
  3. Participate in the discussion, activity, or lab.
  4. Finish any follow-up work at home.

What to Bring

  • Biology notebook or binder
  • Pencil or pen
  • Current weekly packet
  • Completed reading notes or worksheet
  • Questions from the week’s work

Course Habits

Students should practice careful reading, following directions, recording observations, defining vocabulary, completing tables, and writing short explanations. These habits matter just as much as memorizing biology terms.
