# Link, QR Code, and Parent Screening Policy

Linked videos and web resources may be used to support weekly biology topics. These resources are selected with the intent of being useful and age-appropriate, but parents should preview videos and links according to their family’s standards before student use. Parents make the final decision about whether a video, website, or supplemental resource is appropriate for their student. Optional resources may be skipped, replaced, or used only after parent review.

## Resource Format

Course documents should work well for both printed packets and computer use. Each major online resource should include enough information for students and parents to access it from either paper or screen.

Recommended format:

- Resource title
- Clickable link in the digital version
- Short URL when practical
- QR code for printed packets
- Required or optional label
- Brief parent note only when the topic is sensitive or worldview-related

## Weekly Packet Use

The parent screening statement should appear in this policy and in the course start materials. It does not need to be repeated under every single weekly resource unless a specific resource needs extra context.

Weekly packets may simply include a **This Week’s Resources** box with links, QR codes, and required or optional labels.
