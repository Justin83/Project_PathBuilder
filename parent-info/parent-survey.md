# Parent Survey Draft

This survey is intended to help shape the practical details of the Biology Foundations course before the full 15-week plan is finalized. Parents are assumed to remain the primary graders for weekly assignments unless a separate arrangement is made.

## Access and Printing

1. Does your student have reliable internet access during the week?
2. Does your student have access to a laptop, Chromebook, tablet, or shared computer?
3. Would your family prefer online readings, printed packets, or both?

## Weekly Work and Parent Support

4. How many hours per week can your student reasonably spend on biology outside of the Tuesday meeting?

## Labs and Activities

5. Are you open to occasional campus lab days for microscopes, equipment, or larger activities?
6. Are there allergies, sensitivities, or safety concerns the instructor should know about?

## Online Resources

7. Are you comfortable with YouTube or web links being included as optional or recommended resources when parents have the final say about student use?
8. Would QR codes in printed packets be useful for phone access?
